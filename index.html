Understanding AI Agents

What are AI Agents?

AI Agents are autonomous entities that perceive their environment through sensors and act upon that environment through actuators to achieve specific goals.

Types of AI Agents

Simple Reflex Agents

Act based on current percept, ignoring percept history.

Model-Based Agents

Maintain internal state to track aspects not evident in current percept.

Goal-Based Agents

Act to achieve their goals using search and planning.

Utility-Based Agents

Maximize their own expected "happiness" or utility.

Applications of AI Agents

  • Virtual assistants (Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant)
  • Autonomous vehicles
  • Game AI (NPCs in video games)
  • Fraud detection systems
  • Smart home devices
  • Recommendation systems
